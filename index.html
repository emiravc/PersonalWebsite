My Projects

During my senior year in high school, I took place in a physics project where I completed my first real-world project. My high school had hydroponic/ garden towers; however these towers had some issues with watering the plants. Sometimes these towers wouldn't measure the water level right and it would cost the plants to die. As a solution, I designed a self monitoring IoT plant watering system. This system had a digital screen , alarm system for low/high water levels and also an app that shows the water level with remote access.
